## 3D-printed parts

All parts to be printed in **PETG** (indoor) or **ASA** (outdoor) at
**40 % gyroid infill**, 3 perimeter walls, 0.4 mm nozzle.

| Part | Filament (g, estimate) | Print time (hrs, estimate) | Notes |
|------|------------------------|---------------------------|-------|
| Robot contact block (100×100×50 mm) | 180 g | 3.5 h | Print with contact face down (smooth surface) |
| Floating contact plate (160×160×18 mm) | 220 g | 4 h | |
| Entry funnel (220×180 → 112×112, 80 mm deep) | 380 g | 7 h | May need to split into 2 pieces |
| Tower cover (200×250×120 mm) | 450 g | 9 h | Indoor only; outdoor uses NEMA 4X metal box |
| Back plate (220×220×12 mm) | 260 g | 5 h | Can substitute 6 mm aluminium plate (stronger) |
| SSR bracket (custom to enclosure) | 50 g | 1 h | Mount SSR to metal wall for heat sinking |
| Contact pad pocket inserts (optional jigs) | 30 g | 0.5 h | One-time press-fit jigs for gluing pads |

**Total filament estimate:** ~1.6 kg PETG or ASA.
